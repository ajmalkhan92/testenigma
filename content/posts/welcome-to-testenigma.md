TestEnigma is a blog about two sides of the same coin: using AI to make
software testing better, and testing AI systems themselves so we can
actually trust what they produce.

Expect posts on things like AI-assisted test generation, self-healing test
suites, LLM evaluation, hallucination detection, prompt regression testing,
and the occasional deep dive into a testing concept with a longer history
than the AI hype cycle.

New posts land regularly — stay tuned.
